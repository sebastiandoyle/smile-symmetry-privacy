Privacy Policy

For Smile Symmetry Trainer

Effective date:

Summary

  • No data collection: The app does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.
  • On‑device processing: Smile symmetry is computed locally using Apple ARKit/TrueDepth blend shapes.
  • Camera access: The front camera is used only while the training view is visible.
  • No third‑party analytics or ads: None are integrated.

Data we do not collect

We do not collect names, photos, videos, biometric identifiers, usage analytics, device identifiers, or precise location.

Camera and TrueDepth

The app reads ARKit blend shape coefficients (e.g., mouth smile left/right) to provide symmetry feedback. Frames and depth data are not saved or sent off‑device.

Children

The app is suitable for general audiences and does not contain content targeted specifically at children.
